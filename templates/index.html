Give Me Some Credit Machine Learning Project.

Banks play a crucial role in market economies. They decide who can get finance and on what terms and can make or break the investment decisions. For markets and society to function, individuals and companies need acces to credit. Credit scoring algorithms, which makes a guess at the probability of default, are the methods banks use to determine whether or not a loan should be granted. In the project, we try to improve on the state or the art in credit scoring, by predicting whether a customer will experience financial distress in the next 2 years. The goal of the project is to use historical data on borrowers to predict whether a customer will repay the loan or not (hence becoming a defaulter).
